About Us

Our Story

TGS, also known as The Golden Snubs, was founded over 15 days ago by four friends with a passion for fashion: Hansaint Noto, Carlos Posadas, Abdurrehman Aslam, and Ghazi Harara. Our goal is to sell clothes, but mostly to make money.

Meet the Founders

Hansaint Noto

Hansaint is a fashion enthusiast who loves to keep up with the latest trends. He has a keen eye for design and ensures that every piece of clothing sold by TGS is stylish and on-trend.

Carlos Posadas

Carlos is a marketing genius who knows how to create buzz around a brand. He is responsible for TGS's social media presence and has helped us gain a loyal following in a short amount of time.

Abdurrehman Aslam

Abdurrehman is the numbers guy at TGS. He ensures that our finances are in order and that we are making a profit. He also helps us make strategic business decisions that will help us grow.

Ghazi Harara

Ghazi is a former criminal who loves to make stupid amounts of money. He once started a lemonade stand and tricked 3rd graders to run and operate it for no compensation. He was once accused of robbing the federal reserve but his lawyer has advised him to not speak of it.

Our Success

Since its founding just over 15 days ago, The Golden Snubs (TGS) has quickly become one of the most successful clothing brands on the market. With a focus on trendy, stylish designs and high-quality materials, TGS has built a loyal customer base that continues to grow day by day. In addition to its impressive sales figures, TGS has also received widespread critical acclaim, with fashion experts and influencers alike praising the brand's unique aesthetic and commitment to excellence. Looking ahead, TGS shows no signs of slowing down, and we can't wait to see what the future holds for this exciting and innovative company.
